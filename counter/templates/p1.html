What Is Calorie Counting?

Calorie counting is the method of recording the calorie (or kilojoule) amount of each and every food and drink you consume every day!!

It can be done using a food journal, an app, or by simply recording the information on a spreadsheet.

Alternately calorie-based meal plans can be prepared in advance and then followed exactly.
